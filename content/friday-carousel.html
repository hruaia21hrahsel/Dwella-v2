Slide 2 — What is Rental Yield?
What is rental yield?
The annual return you earn from rent, expressed as a percentage of your property's value.
It tells you whether your property is a smart investment — or dead money sitting in concrete.
Slide 3 — The Formula
THE FORMULA
Gross Rental Yield =
(Annual Rent ÷ Property Value) × 100
Slide 4 — Example
EXAMPLE
Property Value₹80,00,000
Monthly Rent₹25,000
Annual Rent₹3,00,000
(3,00,000 ÷ 80,00,000) × 100 =
3.75%

Slide 6 — Net Yield
Net yield is more accurate
Subtract these from annual rent before calculating:
−
Maintenance charges
−
Property tax
−
Insurance premium
−
Vacancy months (assume 1/year)
−
Repair costs
Net Yield = (Annual Rent − Expenses) ÷ Property Value × 100
