Carousel How to Calculate Rental Yield

Slide-by-Slide Content

SlideText
1 (Cover)How to Calculate Rental Yield on Your Property 📊 — A landlord's most important number
2What is rental yield? The annual return you earn from rent, as a % of property value. It tells you if your property is a good investment or dead money.
3The Formula: Gross Rental Yield = (Annual Rent ÷ Property Value) × 100
4Example: Property: ₹80,00,000 — Monthly rent: ₹25,000 — Annual: ₹3,00,000 — Yield: 3.75%
5Good yield in India? 2-3% Below avg 🔴 | 3-4% Average 🟡 | 4-5% Good 🟢 | 5%+ Excellent ⭐
6Net yield: Subtract maintenance, property tax, insurance, vacancy months, repairs. Net = (Rent - Expenses) ÷ Value × 100
7Improve your yield: Price at market rate • Reduce vacancy • Furnish to charge premium • Track every payment
8 (CTA)Dwella tracks your rent automatically so you never lose yield to missed payments. Waitlist — link in bio.
